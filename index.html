Ingredients:

For the Cake:

  • 200 g Self-Raising Flour
  • 200 g Butter at room temperature
  • 150 g Sugar
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Tsp Baking Powder
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 50 ml Milk
  • 1 Tbsp Vanilla Essence

For the Icing:

  • 200 g Icing Sugar
  • 80 g Butter
  • ¬Ω Sprinkles
  • 1 Tbsp Vanilla Essence

Instructions:

  1. Mix together the butter and sugar, ideally using an electric whisk
  2. Add the eggs and continue to whisk
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  4. Sift in the flour, baking powder, and salt. Fold in gently.
  5. Add the milk and vanilla essence. Mix until well combined.
  6. Spread the mixture evenly into a lightly greased baking tray. I personally use a 30cm by 20cm baking tray, but any size will work great as long as it has space to rise
  7. Place into oven for 25 mins at 180¬∞C. You can extend this time as necessary until a toothpick comes out clean, as dependent on the size of your baking tray it may take longer/less time to cook
  8. Prepare the icing by mixing together all of the ingredients
  9. Evenly spread the icing over the caking after allowing it some time to cool (ideally 20 minutes)
  10. Cover in sprinkles, and enjoy
